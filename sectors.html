Where Odirico Fits

The sector pages are designed to feel premium and scalable now, while leaving room for deeper narratives, project examples, and future subpages later.

Infrastructure

Supporting public works, utilities, and civil project delivery.

Odirico can support infrastructure teams that need reliable coordination, project documentation, and technical organization around active work.

  • Coordination support for public works, utility, and infrastructure programs.
  • Tracking systems for submittals, RFIs, issue logs, and project documentation.
  • Execution support that helps owners, contractors, and project teams stay aligned.
Energy

Project support for power, utility, and facility system work.

The energy sector positioning is intentionally practical, focused on coordination, documentation, and delivery support around upgrades, expansions, and system work.

  • Support for utility, power, and facility-related scopes where structure matters.
  • Equipment tracking and organized technical records for project teams.
  • Documentation systems that help clarify status, responsibilities, and open items.
Construction

Execution-minded support for active job sites and delivery teams.

Construction support is framed around keeping information current, teams aligned, and reporting clear enough to help work move.

  • Field reporting, issue tracking, and document coordination for active projects.
  • Support with submittals, RFIs, punch lists, QA/QC logs, and closeout items.
  • Contractor-facing coordination that keeps communication organized and usable.
Facilities

Technical support for building systems and facility-related projects.

Facilities work benefits from the firm’s systems-oriented positioning and mechanical familiarity without making mechanical design the headline.

  • Support for building systems, mechanical installations, and facility upgrades.
  • Structured documentation around equipment, systems, and coordination needs.
  • Technical clarity for teams managing installations, turnover, or operational readiness.
