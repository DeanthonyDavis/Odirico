Market 01

Power and utilities

Electric cooperatives, municipal utilities, and owner-operators are a strong first fit because inspection, reporting, and field execution quality are tied directly to risk.

  • Utility structures, field support, and inspection-adjacent work.
  • Documentation expectations that benefit from better process discipline.
  • Natural overlap with the PoleQA product direction.
Market 02

Telecom and broadband

Broadband deployment and telecom field programs often combine site activity, reporting complexity, and contractor coordination in ways that fit the Odirico model well.

  • Field documentation that is clean enough for program oversight.
  • Support for teams managing dispersed work and reporting handoffs.
  • Useful alignment with grant-funded and compliance-sensitive programs.
Market 03

Contractors and EPC teams

Contractors need help when communication, reporting, and active field tracking start slipping between teams, scopes, and deadlines.

  • Project services that support active delivery teams.
  • Issue visibility, closeout structure, and execution reporting.
  • Software upside for repeat inspection and PM workflows.
Market 04

Public infrastructure and agency programs

City, county, and grant-backed programs often need outside support that can help teams document work properly and communicate it clearly.

  • Good fit for capability-statement-led outreach.
  • Useful where compliance and public accountability matter.
  • Strong long-term path once registrations and procurement profiles are in place.
