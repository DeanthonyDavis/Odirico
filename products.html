Module 01 · PoleQA

Inspection and QA/QC for utility structure workflows.

PoleQA is the first live module in Odirico OS. It is built for issue capture, review cycles, and documentation-heavy field inspection work.

Open PoleQA
Tickets

QA/QC ticketing

  • Ticket-first workflow for findings, review, and closeout.
  • Dashboard, list, and kanban-oriented organization.
  • Role-aware visibility for teams working the same scope.
Documentation

Field reporting

  • Structured issue capture tied to real project records.
  • Workflow support for field observations and revisions.
  • Clear handoff from field discovery to corrective action.
Operations

Throughput visibility

  • Status tracking across submissions, QA, rework, and approval.
  • Insight into bottlenecks and outstanding issues.
  • Useful for both internal teams and client-facing reporting.
Module 02 · PM Platform
